Our Services

Operational support across the revenue cycle — designed to reduce administrative burden and increase clarity.

Revenue Cycle Management

A structured approach across front, middle, and back office activities. We focus on clean workflows, consistent follow‑up, and measurable improvement.

  • Eligibility + authorization support
  • Charge capture and claim readiness checks
  • A/R follow‑up and payer escalation
  • Monthly performance review and planning

Medical Billing & Coding

Accurate coding and disciplined claim management to keep denials low and reimbursement predictable.

  • Coding review and documentation alignment
  • Clean claim submission and tracking
  • Payment posting and reconciliation
  • Secondary claims and corrected claims

Credentialing & Enrollment

Payer credentialing is slow — we make it less painful with checklists, tracking, and complete documentation.

  • CAQH maintenance, payer enrollment, re‑credentialing
  • NPI / TIN / EDI paperwork support
  • Portal setup and payer communications

Denial Prevention & Management

We don’t just “work denials.” We hunt patterns and fix root causes so they stop repeating.

  • Denial reason analysis and categorization
  • Appeals support and payer follow‑up
  • Workflow + documentation fixes

Practice Analytics & Forecasting

Clear reporting that helps you make decisions: staffing, payer strategy, and operational focus.

  • A/R aging visibility
  • Denial and underpayment tracking
  • Monthly forecasting and action items

Patient Intake, Scheduling & Reminders

Support to streamline patient communication and reduce no‑shows.

  • Intake workflow design
  • Appointment reminders (text/email/phone workflows)
  • Front‑desk operational support
