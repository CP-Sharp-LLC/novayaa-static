OUR PROCESS

Since the business environment has been greater complicated ahead of several obstacles, however, Novayaa adopted a comprehensive approach to measuring the performance of small and large practices. Our process runs through the following phases:

Credentialing
Credentialing of a provider with Government, Commercial and Private Entities is a little time taking process however, getting a claim paid is impossible unless the provider or practice is firmly identified by the payer. Available Out of network benefits is another option to have the claim paid.
Charge Entry
The charge Entry process has a vital role in the medical billing cycle. Novayaa ensures that entered information in the claims is flawless and error-free.
Payment Posting
Payment posting is such an important part of an efficient medical billing system. Payments are posted in the patients’ accounts. The team also takes care of adjustments, write-offs, denied and rejected claims, and more like secondary billing and corrected claims.
A/R Follow Up
Novayaa has volunteers who are technically sound and very well aware of all possible denials received from carriers. Smart work and quick response to a denied claim is their core obligation. So the team gets reimbursements and makes the practice prosper.
Denial Management
Management of denials is a decisive part of the revenue cycle. Healthy cash flow and successful revenue could not be attained until having an efficient denial fixation system. Our staff easily determine the reason for denials and also tries to reduce the risk of upcoming denials. Finally, we achieve maximum reimbursements.
Reporting
Reports are considered to show the performance of a practice in a nutshell. Novayaa generates a wide array of reports for the clients to reflect various aspects of their practices. Daily, Weekly, Biweekly, Monthly, and Annual Reports are given to the clients accordingly.
